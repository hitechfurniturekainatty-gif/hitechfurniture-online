Homepage visual direction: present Hitech Furniture & Interiors as a guided walk through one ultra-luxury modern villa in daylight.

Rules:
- Use the uploaded hero imagery in this fixed sequence: living room, dining room, kitchen, master bedroom, balcony, complete villa reveal.
- Visual palette should stay bright and premium: white stone, warm beige, champagne highlights, and walnut wood tones.
- The homepage should feel cinematic and architectural, with Apple/Tesla-style restraint rather than generic furniture-store cards.
- Motion should suggest a walkthrough: slow zooms, layered parallax, soft reveal timing, and smooth sectional transitions.
- Typography should feel editorial and premium, with a refined serif display face and a clean modern sans for body copy.
- Keep the experience mobile-first, fast-loading, and polished, with imagery doing most of the storytelling.
